Research Interest

The main research focus lies on the solar wind and large-scale solar wind structures (coronal mass ejections, stream ineraction regions, high-speed streams) and their influence on the Earth's magnetosheath and magnetosphere. In particular, he investigates the influence of the solar wind on dynamic pressure enhancements ("jets") in the magnetosheath. The broader research focus includes stellar activity and solar- terrestrial relations in general.
I am, part of the Heliospheric Research Group at the University of Graz.
